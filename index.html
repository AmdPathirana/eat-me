About Us

Who are we?

"Eat Me's" aims to indulge every client with a truely memorable experience through our high quality foods and beverages and our attention to detail, and our continued commitment to the highest possible level of customer service. At Eat Me, we are passionate about our food. Simply, it means we are committed to always evolving what matters to you.

We were first established in 2005. Since in the implimentation Eat Me set the standard as far as meals goes, introducing variety of delicious new foods with alternative ingredients such as seafood, mushroom, pepperoni and other exciting ingredients. Until then ordinary meals were usually served.

Eat Me is somewhat of a pioneer when it comes to modernizing the food culture. We able to provide perfect and authentic food experience to our customers using our Mains, Staters, Beverages as well as Desserts. It is a pleasure to maintaining quality, consistency and great value for money, as you would expect from us. We take great care that what we serve every day is safe, great quality offers choice and is produced in a responsible way.
